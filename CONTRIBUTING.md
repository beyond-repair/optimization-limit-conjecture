# Contributing to Optimization-Limit Conjecture (𝒲)

## Development Philosophy
Hierarchical epistemic rigor: Level 1 (analytic invariants) before Level 2 (information-theoretic mappings) and Level 3 (topology).

## How to Contribute
1. **Issues**: Report bugs, suggest parameter extensions, or propose cross-domain mappings.
2. **Pull Requests**: 
   - Fork → Branch (feature/ or proof/).
   - Ensure experiments pass and align with CONJECTURE.md.
   - Update proofs/ for new theorems.
3. **Standards**: All claims distinguished (computational / conjectural / proved). Use LaTeX in proofs/.

## Areas of Focus
- Theorem B/C formalization
- Rate-distortion extensions (docs/)
- General graph topologies (beyond trees)
